Our Story

Born From a Simple Belief

Founded in Salem, Tamil Nadu in 2021, Stackly began with a simple belief: every home deserves intelligent automation that works seamlessly with daily life — not against it.

Our founders noticed that smart home technology was either too complex, too expensive or simply not designed for Indian homes. Stackly was created to change that — combining cutting-edge AI with elegant, practical design.

Today we serve thousands of homeowners across Tamil Nadu and beyond, delivering solutions that genuinely improve daily life — from automated lighting and climate control to advanced security and energy management.

FAQs

Frequently Asked
Questions

How long does installation take?
A standard home installation typically takes 1–2 days. For larger properties or custom setups, our team will provide an accurate timeline during the free consultation.
Do I need to replace all my existing devices?
Not at all! Stackly integrates seamlessly with most existing appliances and smart devices including Amazon Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit and 500+ IoT devices.
What happens if I lose internet connectivity?
Our systems have local fallback mode — core functions like lights, locks and automation schedules continue working without internet. Cloud features like remote access and alerts require connectivity.
Is there a monthly subscription fee?
Yes, we offer flexible plans starting at ₹999/month that include cloud access, AI automation, remote monitoring and 24/7 support. Hardware is a one-time purchase.
